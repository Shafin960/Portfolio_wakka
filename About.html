Hi, I'm Wakif

During my sophomore year as a social science student, I stumbled upon something that changed everything—design. What started as an interest in logos and branding led me to the world of User Experience Design, where I quickly became captivated by how design can shape the way people interact with the world around them.

In my final year of university, I entered a UX competition hosted by the Software Engineering Department and earned 2nd runner-up in the UX Design segment. That moment sparked a fire in me—a drive to learn and grow more in the field of UX design.
